Introduction to NEP2020
- National Education Policy 2020 (NEP2020) is a comprehensive framework to transform India's education system
- Launched by the Government of India to address the changing needs of the 21st century
- NEP2020 aims to increase access to quality education for all citizens
- Focuses on equity, inclusivity, and affordability
- Relevant now due to the rapidly changing educational landscape in India

Current Use Cases in Government Offices
- Implementation of digital literacy programs for government employees, such as the National Digital Literacy Mission
- Utilization of online platforms for grievance redressal, like the Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System (CPGRAMS)
- Data analysis and visualization for informed decision-making, using tools like the Government Data Portal (data.gov.in)
- Conducting online training sessions for government officials, such as the National Institute of Administrative Research (NIAR) programs
